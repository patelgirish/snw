About S&W

Resolving Critical Industry Challenges & Measurably Improving Financial Performance

“Stern & Wise” is founded in Delhi NCR to provide management consulting services in Energy and Infrastructure industries. The strategy of the firm is being planned to expand across all major industries and regions of India by providing – Strategy, Consulting, Digital & Technology Implementation solutions.

At Stern & Wise, we love to –
  • – resolve emerging policy and regulatory bottlenecks
  • – implement Small Changes leading to Big Impacts
  • – enhance decision making through consumer awareness
  • – learn and innovate continuously for enabling people and processes, future-ready


The S&W Story

We believe there are better ways of Problem Solving and We Know, We Can do it differently.

Stern & Wise was founded by Girish Patel in April 2018, after passionate journey of around two decades across industry, consulting and information technology. Having successfully dealt with unique challenges at each stage of his career, Girish decided to resolve the corporate anxieties in unconventional way and took this entrepreneurial opportunity.

Where Did the Name “Stern & Wise” Come from?

In recent past, the Indian business fraternity has been witnessing toughening rules and regulations. While sustaining through these norms requires uncompromised internal and external compliances, it also entails keeping abreast of dynamics and being judicious in decision making. Inspired from this situation, thoughtfully emerged “Stern & Wise Management Consulting”.
