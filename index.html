About Us!

Shiv Gayatri Construction Co. came into being with a clear vision - to become a global leader in the Delhi NCR of Upvc Windows & Doors.
It was originally engaged in carrying full-fledged Installation of windows and doors for various different company in Delhi NCR, India. We manufacture high quality uPVC windows, doors and conservatory products RIGHT HERE IN SHEFFIELD. Over the years, we have built up a reputation of being a company that you can trust, offering honest advice and first class customer service. Today with the help of professional technical expertise, our company started promoting the Upvc Doors Windows, Aluminium Doors & Windows and Glass Fittings in several brand.
